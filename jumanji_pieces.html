Abstract

Recently we got the board game Jumanji , which came in a cool wooden box, but the pieces included are somewhat disappointing (just standard colored game pieces).

I figured I could do one better by making my own pieces that are closer to the ones in the movie.

Modeling

I have a ZIP archive of the Jumanji board and pieces in STL format. Unfortunately, I can't find the original free source online. I belive it was SketchFab or somewhere similar.

Printing & Finishing

Using the STL files for the player pieces, I printed them in Hatchbox Wood filament at 0.1mm layer height.
